Section 01

General Policy

All purchases on Whatt??? are for digital services consumed in real time. As a general rule:

Section 02

Eligible Refunds

We will consider refund requests in the following circumstances:

  • Duplicate charge: Your account was charged more than once for the same transaction
  • Technical failure: A call failed to connect or terminated prematurely due to a platform-side error, and no service was delivered
  • Unauthorized transaction: A charge was made on your account without your authorization (subject to investigation)
  • Service not delivered: A paid feature was not activated or accessible due to a verified system error
All refund requests are subject to investigation and must be supported with relevant transaction IDs and a description of the issue.
Section 03

Non-Refundable Items

  • Completed call sessions, regardless of outcome or user satisfaction
  • Wallet credits or top-ups that have been fully or partially used
  • Account suspension or termination due to policy violations
  • Subscription fees for periods already in use
  • Promotional credits, bonuses, or gifted amounts
Refunds will not be issued for dissatisfaction with other users' behavior. Please use the in-app report feature to flag conduct issues.
